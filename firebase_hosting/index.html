        How Money Works

        Win payout formula: payout = bet_amount x live_odds x payout_multiplier.
        Net win = payout - bet_amount.
        Loss = -bet_amount.
        Coupons apply directly to wallet balance.

        User Guide

        1) Pick endpoint option. Selected row is tried first. If it fails, it turns red and app tries next row automatically.
        2) For other computers use ngrok wss://.... Localhost only works on your own machine.
        3) Place bet during countdown for current race. During racing/post-race, bet is queued for next race.
        4) Use Cancel Bet to remove current/queued bet before settlement.
        5) Public mode: admin/rig controls are fully hidden.
        6) Sound and music need one click (browser policy) before audio plays.
